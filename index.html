About Seoluna International

Established in 2022, Seoluna International brings Korean skincare excellence to global brands. We partner with accredited Korean labs and manufacturers to deliver clean, effective formulas at scale ‚Äî tailored for B2B partners and private-label launches.

Our Philosophy

We combine gentle, proven actives with modern Korean R&D to formulate products that are safe, stable and market-ready. Sustainability and transparency guide our ingredient choices and packaging options.

R&D & Formulation

Formulas are developed with leading Korean labs and dermatologists. Each SKU undergoes preservative efficacy, stability and microbial testing to meet international standards.

Wholesale & Private Label

Seoluna operates on wholesale terms ‚Äî structured MOQs, case-packed SKUs and long-term supply partnerships. We do not fulfil single-piece retail orders; our systems are optimised for volume and quality.

Korean Manufacturing Partners

Our partner facilities in South Korea utilise automated lines, GMP practices and extensive QA processes ‚Äî enabling consistent, export-ready production for global markets.

Automated Filling

High-speed filling, capping and inspection lines suitable for essences, serums and creams to support large-batch manufacturing.

QC & Lab Testing

In-house QC labs perform microbial, stability and preservative efficacy testing on every batch prior to export release.

Packaging & Artwork

Private-label artwork, collapsible cartons and sustainable packaging options are available. We assist with labeling compliance per destination market.

Supply Chain & Export

Our logistics partners manage export documentation, customs clearance and consolidated shipping. We offer CIF/FOB terms and support with HS codes and labelling requirements.
